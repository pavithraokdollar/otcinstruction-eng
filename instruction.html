OK$ Money Withdrawal from Bank

  • 1. To withdraw cash, first need to generate "Bank Withdraw Code"
  • 2. Visit Nearest Bank branch and fill the data completely to generate "Bank Withdraw Code"
  • 3. Inform Bank Counter staff that you want to withdraw amount from OK$
  • 4. show/Tell the code to the Bank Staff
  • 5. Afterthat, Inform the OTP that has been generated in the OK$ app
  • 6. After post successful verification, the amount is physically given to the OK$ customers by the UAB bank staff
  • 7. Need to Refresh & Log in to OK$ account to check the actual new balance post successful debit
  • 8. If you do not receive OK$ amount please contact us
